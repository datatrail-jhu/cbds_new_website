Donating to the Cloud-Based Data Science Plus Program?

Cloud-Based Data Science Plus (CBDS+) is a non-profit program that runs on the generous donations made to the program and the course fees paid to access our massive open online courses on Leanpub. We would gratefully appreciate donations to enhance the impact of our program. The tutoring program provides learners additional funding and an inexpensive laptop (approx. $500) so they can complete the program.

Your donations will go toward providing free training and laptops to our target population. Any amount you donate will be helpful but you can also consider the following amounts:

With your donation of $10 we can buy bus passes for the learners.

With your donation of $100 we can get home internet access for one student during the period of the program.

With your donation of $500 we can buy a laptop for one learner.

With your donation of $3,000 we can provide in-person training and stipend for one learner.

With your donation of $15,000 we can provide in-person training and stipend for an entire cohort of 5 learners.
