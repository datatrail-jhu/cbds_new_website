How can I use CBDS for my class?

CBDS program is entirely online and students can take it for free or pay-what-you-want. Students get a certificate for each CBDS online class from Leanpub. We have been contacted by other educators about how they can use our resources or replicate the CBDS+ program for their students and we are absolutely thrilled by it. If you are interested in replicating the CBDS+ program in your city, please check our starter kit.

Click here to read the starter kit!
